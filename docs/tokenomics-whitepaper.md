# Goldun ($GLDN) Tokenomics Framework (Institutional Draft)

## Token Utility
1. Governance voting
2. Performance fee discounts
3. Arbitrage pool participation
4. Liquidity incentives
5. Settlement layer for mineral contracts

## Supply Structure
- Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 GLDN
- Allocation:
  - 35% Arbitrage Liquidity
  - 20% Treasury
  - 15% Institutional Investors
  - 10% Team (4-year vest)
  - 10% Strategic Partners
  - 10% Ecosystem Incentives

## Revenue Model
Protocol revenue sources:
- Arbitrage spreads
- Vault fees
- Issuance fees
- Mineral settlement contracts

Revenue distribution framework:
- 50% Token buyback
- 20% Treasury
- 20% Share pool distribution
- 10% Operations
